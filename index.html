Platform Guided Show

Round 2
Project Nova
02
The Missing Document
Goal: Test the email functionality. A required document is missing: see how the platform handles it and how it comes back in.
1

Completeness check flags a gap

The platform detects a missing document after triage. A red flag appears in the dossier: the declaration is incomplete.

2

Trigger the outbound email

The platform drafts an email to the client requesting the missing document. Review, adjust if needed, and send.

3

Response comes back in

When the client replies with the document, the platform automatically links it to the open dossier.

4

Dossier updates automatically

The completeness flag clears. Extracted fields update. The declaration is ready to proceed.

Watch for: How the dossier status changes in real-time as the reply comes in. This is the key workflow moment.

Next Steps & Questions

🧪 Your role as tester

Do as many declarations as possible over the next 3 weeks. You're not just testing: you're building the baseline that proves the value of this platform.

📊 How to log

Use the Notion form after each session. Time-box your work (1-3 hrs), then add one row: how many declarations, total time, overall feel. Not per declaration.

🎫 Tickets & feedback

Something broken or unclear? Log it via our interim ticketing tool (not JIRA yet: that comes later). We track everything and prioritise weekly.

🏢 Your champion role at go-live

At go-live (July 6, your office), you train your colleagues: the same way you were trained today. Be present on go-live day. Rene coordinates.

Questions? Raise them now, or reach out directly to Ole, Marco or Maarten. We're here throughout the testing period.
